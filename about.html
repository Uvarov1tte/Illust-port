ABOUT ME

Hi and nice to meet you! I'm Alm4nditte, or can be known as Chi Nguyen, a Viet engineering student, an aspiring developer and a hobbyist freelance artist, currently based in Finland. Most of my works currently focus on some of the characters I find interesting and I'm trying my best to improve my skills while balancing it with my uni life.

I love working with colors and lighting as you can see, and hope you can enjoy my works as much as I love creating them :)
